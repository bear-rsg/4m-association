Final 4M Governing Council

Date 13-08-2009

This final gathering of the consortium proceeded as per the planned agenda.

During the first day the Partners were introduced to the EUMINAfab Infrastructure Project and the possibilities it presents for the 4M community to continue to work together.

In the evening our hosts at FZK/KIT had arranged a superb meal to which we were happy to welcome Prof. Wolfgang Menz who had served our network as Chairman. To show our thanks for his service Stefan Dimov made a presentation to Wolfgang of an engraved clock.

On day two, the final Governing Council was held. The minutes for this are available to Partners here. Partners were also introduced to the FP7 projects that have emerged from the 4M network, namely FlexPAET, COTECH and Multilayer.

26 of the 30 Partner organisations were represented which, after 4 years and 9 months, illustrates the commitment that has been shown to the 4M Network of Excellence by those involved.
