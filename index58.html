Micro-sandblasting

Micro sandblasting uses air and micro particles to create structures on a micrometric scale. With this technique it is also possible to create holes when either the substrate is very thin (foil) or the method is applied longer. The geometry of created structures is defined by masks.

ion etching

Reactive ion etching (RIE) is an etching technology used in microfabrication. It uses chemically reactive plasma to remove material deposited on wafers. The plasma is generated under low pressure (vacuum) by an electromagnetic field. High-energy ions from the plasma attack the wafer surface and react with it.
