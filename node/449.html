4M/ICOMM2009 accepted and indexed by Thomson Reuters'

We are pleased to announce that 4M/ICOMM Conference 2009 has been accepted for inclusion within the Conference Proceedings Citation Index. The 4M/ICOMM Conference 2009 was one of the first conferences to be published via the Engineering Conferences Online (ECO) website.
