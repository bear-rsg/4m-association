Call for Papers: The 4th International Conference on Manipulation, Manufacturing and Measurement on the Nanoscale

October 27-31st, 2014 | Taipei, Taiwan

3M-NANO is the annual International Conference on Manipulation, Manufacturing and Measurement on the Nanoscale; it will be held in 2014 in Taipei, Taiwan. The ultimate ambition of this conference series is to bridge the gap between nanosciences and engineering sciences, aiming at technology opportunities and emerging markets. Scientists working in research fields related to 3M-NANO topics are invited to submit papers. All accepted full papers (presented at the conference and following IEEE format) will be included in IEEE Xplore database and indexed by Ei Compendex. Read more>>
