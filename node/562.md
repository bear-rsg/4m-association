## 4M2011 - Stuttgart, Germany, 8-10th November


![4M2011](/4m-association/assets/images/4m-2011_web1.jpg)
Sixty-seven papers have been accepted for publication in the 4M2011 Proceedings, which are now in press. All those who wish to attend should [Register](/4m-association/conference/2011/Registration-fees) as soon as possible. Six [invited speakers](/4m-association/conference/2011/Invited-Speakers-.md) have now been confirmed and twelve [sponsors](/conference/2011/Our-Sponsors.md) have signed up. We look forward to meeting you all in Stuttgart.
