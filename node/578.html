History of the 4M Conference Series

This series of conferences on multi-material micro-manufacture is organised by the 4M Association.

Past 4M conferences

  • 4M2005 - Forschungszentrum Karlsruhe, Germany
  • 4M2006 - The European Centre for Micro and Nano Technology, Grenoble, France
  • 4M2007 - Borovets, Bulgaria
  • 4M2008 - Cardiff University, United Kingdom
  • 4M/ICOMM 2009 - Karlsruhe, Germany
  • ICOMM/4M 2010 - Madison, Wisconsin, USA
  • 4M2010 - Bourg en Bresse and Oyonnax, France
  • 4M2011- Stuttgart, Germany,
  • 4M2012- Vienna, Austria
  • 4M2013- San Sebastian, Spain
  • 4M/ICOMM2015- Milan, Italy
  • 4M/IWMF2016- Lyngby, Denmark
  • WCMNM2018- Portorož, Slovenia
  • WCMNM2019- Raleigh, USA.html
