Step by Step Guide

  1. Go to the conference submission website
  2. To submit a paper follow the Submit paper link
  3. fill in the form, ensuring all required information is present and correct
  4. make sure you remember your password. Each paper is given a paper id and password, which allows all authors to update the submission information or re-upload the paper
  5. the submitted paper must be in PDF format
  6. Make sure you follow the template of the sample paper which can be downloaded by clicking on the icon below.
  7. the total number of pages should not exceed four(4)
  8. submit the paper by 19th March, 2012 deadline
  9. If required, update the submission information, like author details, by following the Edit Submission link
  10. If required, for example after review, you can re-upload the paper, using the Re-Upload Paper link
  11. If your paper is accepted with revisions, re-upload the revised paper as above, but as a .doc file, by 8th June, 2012
  12. Submit the final version of the paper and send us the copyright forms by end of July, 2012

Note: All authors will be asked to review papers and will be automatically entered into the reviewer pool for the conference. We will send further reviewer guidelines
