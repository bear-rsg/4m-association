BMG: Brittle Or Ductile

In a paper published online in the journal Nature Communications, a Yale University team and collaborators propose a way of predicting whether a given glass will be brittle or ductile — a desirable property typically associated with metals like steel or aluminium — and assert that any glass could have either quality.
