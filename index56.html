ICOMM 2011

The 6th International Conference on MicroManufacturing (ICOMM 2011) will be held on the Tokyo Denki University campus in Tokyo, Japan.

It will take place from March 7th to March 10th, 2011.

Abstracts are due October 15, 2010.

Full papers are due January 15, 2011.
