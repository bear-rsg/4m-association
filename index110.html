ion etching

Reactive ion etching (RIE) is an etching technology used in microfabrication. It uses chemically reactive plasma to remove material deposited on wafers. The plasma is generated under low pressure (vacuum) by an electromagnetic field. High-energy ions from the plasma attack the wafer surface and react with it.

Laser drilling

Laser drilling is a non-cutting machining method. The energy input by Laser is locally so high that the material ionises and evaporates. This plasma leaves the substrate and leaves a hole. The biggest advantages of this technique are the contactless machining and that the temperature stays very low.
