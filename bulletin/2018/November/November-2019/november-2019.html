WCMNM2019, Call for papers

The 2019 World Congress on Micro and Nano Manufacturing will take place from 10th to 12th September 2019 in Raleigh NC, USA. It is jointly organised by the International Institution for Micro Manufacturing (I2M2), the 4M Association and the International Forum on Micro Manufacturing (IFMM).

Conference Chair

Gracious Ngaile, North Carolina State University, USA

Conference Co-Chairs

Paul Cohen, North Carolina State University, USA

Sathyan Subbiah, IIT Madras, India

Stefan Dimov, University of Birmingham, UK

Kuniaki Dohda, Northwestern University, USA

Scientific Committee Chairs

Lawrence Kulinky, University of california, USA

Steffen G. Scholz, Karlsruhe Institute of Technology, Germany

Tohru Sasaki, University of Toyama, Japan

Topics of Interest for Call for Papers (but not limited to)

  • Micro fabrication technologies, process chains and process characterisatio
  • Miniaturization of machines and equipment as well as associated issues such as tooling, fixturing, positioning, motion generation, sensors systems, and control
  • Novel product designs, micro-assembly technologies and micro-handlin
  • Surface engineering and interface nanotechnolog
  • Process modelling and simulatio
  • Processing and characterisation of smart materials, multifunctional materials, nanomaterials and material related issues in micro and nano scal
  • Micro and nano additive manufacturing technologie
  • Micro and desktop factory concepts, systems, components and module
  • On-line monitoring and inspection systems/method
  • Standardization in micro manufacturing and micro factorie
  • Applications of micro and nano technologies: microreactor technologies, microsensors and actuator
  • Applications of both current and emerging micromanufacturing methods and equipment, including those that bridge the nano- and macro- worlds

Important Dates

The main congress programme takes place from 10th to the 12th of September 2019

Submission site will open: 15th of February 2019

Submission deadline for full papers: 15th of April 2019

Contact: Dr. Gracious Ngaile, North Carolina State University, USA.
